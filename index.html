Air Force PFA Calculator

This calculator is an unofficial tool which can be used to score your Air Force Physical Fitness Assessment (PFA).

This calculator CANNOT be used for the Air Force Academy Candidate Fitness Assessment (CFA). The Air Force Academy CFA is strucutered much differently than the PFA.

Getting your score is as simple as filling in your gender, age, and test results in the fields below, and clicking on Submit. Your score breakdown and Composite score will be shown at the bottom of the app.
